The Art of Unexpected.

A special numbered edition limited to 630 + 63 units of the new Panigale V4. Inspired by the Lamborghini Revuelto Ad Personam, this collector’s model is the third chapter in the collaboration between these two global Made in Italy brand ambassadors. Complete with special livery, carbon fibre elements, a dry clutch and Akrapovič silencer, this is the new Panigale V4 Lamborghini.

The perfect alchemy, from the heart of Motor Valley

The third chapter of a collaboration between two brands that share the values of style, performance and engineering excellence. Following on from the Diavel 1260 Lamborghini and Ducati Streetfighter V4 Lamborghini, Panigale V4 Lamborghini is another bike that will capture the imagination of fans at first glance, blending technology, performance and beauty as if by magic.

This latest episode stars the most sophisticated and high-performance models in the brands’ product ranges, the Panigale V4 S, the latest generation Ducati Superbike, and the Lamborghini Revuelto, the new reference in the world of super sports cars. The two top-of-line vehicles are equipped with the finest, most high-performance engines, the very embodiment of the technical philosophies and design approach: the Ducati Desmosedici Stradale engine and the Lamborghini V12.

Bike Specs

  • Displacement: 1 103 cc
  • Power: 218.5 hp (160 kW) @ 13 500 rpm
  • Torque: 122,1 Nm @ 11 250 rpm
  • Seat Height: 850 mm

Presenting the new Panigale V4 Lamborghini

The presentation event - The Art of Unexpected - took place in Teatro Alcione, Milan, during Milan Design Week, the leading event in the design world. The event was attended by a select group of guests, including the Ducati and Lamborghini management teams, as well as a number of influencers and media representatives. The event was hosted by the famous Italian TV presenter and journalist, Andrea Delogu.
