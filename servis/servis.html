Frequently asked questions

How often does a Ducati engine need to be serviced?

Ducati recommends regular servicing every 12,000 km or 12 months, depending on the model. For exact intervals, please check your motorcycle's service booklet or contact us.

Can I have a non-Ducati motorcycle serviced by you?

Yes, we service all brands of motorcycles. Our technicians have extensive knowledge and experience with various brands, but we specialize in Ducati.

How long does the service take?

Regular service usually takes 1-2 business days, depending on the scope of work and the busyness of the service. For larger interventions, we will inform you of the estimated time when we receive the engine.

Do you also offer home pickup of the motorcycle?

Yes, for an additional fee we offer a service of picking up and delivering the motorcycle to your home or desired location. Contact us for more information.
